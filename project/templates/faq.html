Frequently Asked Questions

Account Creation

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click here. Then click on Create a New Account and fill out your profile information. Only the username and password are required, you may choose to leave out your age, country and about me blurb.

Logging In And Out

How do I log in?

To login, click here. Enter your username and password then click Login.

Why can't I log in?

If you are having problems logging in, wait a minute then try again and make sure your username and password are correct.
Note that while you are able to log in on the desktop site, it is recommended that you log in through the mobile site for full functionality.

How do I log out?

To log out, click here. Or you can click Logout from any page on the top header

Creating a Lobby

How do I create a lobby?

Click on Create a Lobby to create a lobby. Creating a lobby generates a random five digit Lobby Code - share this code with your friends so that they can join your lobby.

I can't create a lobby!

Lobby creation is only supported on the desktop site.

Joining a Lobby

How do I join a lobby?

You can join a lobby from your profile. First, you have to log in. Once you have successfully logged in, enter a five digit lobby code in the text box and click Join Lobby.

How come I can't join a lobby?

Make sure you entered the lobby code correctly. Lobbies will expire once all users have left the lobby.

Where do I get the lobby code from?

If your friend has created a lobby, they can share the lobby code for others to join. You can also create a lobby yourself by clicking here.

Gameplay

How does the game work?

The game has four players. The first four users to join the lobby with be the players. Additional users will be added as spectators. The players will have a virtual controller on their phones. Drag on the left side of the screen to use the virtual joystick to move your character around. Tap on the right side of the screen to make your character jump.

Chatting

How do I chat about the game?

Spectators in a game will have access to a group chat application. You can send messages to the group that will be shown next to game.

Why don't I have a chat application?

Only spectators will have the chat application. Spectators are determined by order of joining the lobby, the first four users to join are players, and the rest are spectators.

How do I send messages?

Enter your message in the chat application and click Send. Your message will then appear in the chat window next to the game!
